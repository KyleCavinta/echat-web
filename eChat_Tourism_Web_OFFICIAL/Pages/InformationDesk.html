What is eChat

The SHTM 9th Skills Festival entitled eCHAT: eConvention in Hospitality And Tourism with the theme: “Moving Towards the Next Normal” is a five-day event consisting of exciting and informative activities such as webinars and break-out sessions, as well as talk shows on industry trends before, during and after the pandemic, featuring relevant insights from distinguished speakers and experts from the industry and skills competitions. The event aims to showcase the skills and creativity of students in various hospitality and tourism – related competitions and intends to provide opportunities for the students to gain more knowledge and abilities from the professionals in the field of Tourism and Hospitality. Students and guests from different universities across the country will be invited to attend this event.
